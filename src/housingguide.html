Frequently Asked Questions

We accept multiple payment methods including bank transfers, online payments, and mobile money. You can pay in installments or full semester payments. All transactions are secure and verified.

After finding a property you like, schedule an inspection, submit your application with required documents, pay the security deposit, and complete the lease agreement. The entire process typically takes 3-7 days.

Was this helpful?

Cancellation policies vary by property. Generally, you can cancel within 48 hours of booking for a full refund. After that, cancellation fees may apply. Check the specific property's cancellation policy before booking.

Was this helpful?

Use our mobile app or website to report maintenance issues. Include photos and detailed descriptions. Emergency issues are addressed within 24 hours, while routine maintenance typically takes 3-5 business days.

Was this helpful?

Security deposits are typically equivalent to one month's rent. They are refundable at the end of your lease, subject to property inspection. Deductions may be made for damages beyond normal wear and tear.

Was this helpful?

Most student accommodations offer semester-based leases (4-6 months) or full academic year leases (9-10 months). Some properties also offer monthly rentals with higher rates. Choose based on your academic schedule.
